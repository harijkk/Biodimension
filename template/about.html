ABOUT US

We, at Biodimensions, are the light at the end of the tunnel for the beauty and medical industries' cruel animal testing. We developed a humane approach to animal testing utilizing 4D technology and were recognized as one of the top six startups by the Humane Entrepreneurship Program.

Our purpose here at Biodimensions is to upgrade bio-industries with 4D bioproducts to deliver faster and more affordable gold standards for product testing and development. Our aim is to be a market leader in providing bioprinted organs for industrial and patient usage. Redefining research in a more humane way is our motto, we believe that non-animal alternatives should be used to eradicate animal experimentation for medical industries from the research ecosystem.
