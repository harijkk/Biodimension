BIODCORNEA

Our 4D bioprinted corneal substitutes enables personalized corneal implant with controllable structure and refractive ability.

Normal human corneal epithelial cells are cultivated to form a stratified, squamous epithelium that closely resembles normal human corneal tissue in BioDimensions BioDcornea. The corneal cells differentiate into a multi-layered structure after being cultivated on specially prepared cell culture inserts with serum-free media.

TECHNOLOGY

Eye Irritation Test(OECD TG 492)

By measuring the cytotoxic effect of a topically applied chemical or formulation on the EpiOcular cornea epithelial model, the EpiOcular Eye Irritation Test predicts the acute eye irritation potential of a chemical or formulation. Chemicals (substances) can be identified using this in vitro hazard assessment
