BIODSKIN

Our Skin models provide unique advantages to your product testing over the existing models

BioDskin is an in vitro reconstructed human epidermis made from normal human keratinocytes cultivated in a chemically specified medium on an inert polycarbonate filter at the air-liquid interface.

The BioDskin from Biodimension is made up of real human epidermal keratinocytes. Cultured normal human dermal fibroblasts (NHFB) and normal human dermal fibroblasts (NHEK) are used to create a multilayered, highly distinct human dermis and epidermis model. The NHEK and NHFB cells are grown on specifically designed cell culture inserts.
