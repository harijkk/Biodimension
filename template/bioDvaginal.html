BIODVAGINAL

Our Vaginal models provide unique advantages to your product testing over the existing models

BioDvaginal is an in vitro product that can be used for both feminine product safety testing and HIV-1 and other sexually transmitted diseases research. Its tissue shape and cellular activity are very similar to that of in vivo vaginal epithelial tissue, making it an excellent tool for toxicity and efficacy testing as well as biological study.
